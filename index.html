Introduction

Fiordland, located in the southwestern corner of the South Island of New Zealand, is home of famous Milford and Doubtful Sounds as well as many world famous walking tracks. Fiordland is one of the most dramatic and beautiful parts of New Zealand and is renowned as a Sightseeing and Walking Capital.
Activities and accommodation are centred on four main areas of Fiordland: Te Anau, Manapouri, Milford Sound, Doubtful Sound
